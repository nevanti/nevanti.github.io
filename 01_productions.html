Tracks of Twilight (a working title)

THE TRACKS OF TWILIGHT (2012) - Screenplay based on Asko Sahlberg´s original novel Hämärän Jäljet. People do not meet they collide leaving bruises on each other. This thought occurs to the Man as he gazes into the river late at night, watching the debris, slowly being swept up in the current. His life is going nowhere, but gradually he too will be propelled in a new direction as he is caught up in the ebb and flow of the people around him; Sirpa, Jakobson, Nadya are all strong characters living in the outskirts of the society. Adaptation by Kirsi Nevanti, dialoque by Asko Sahlberg, Script Editor Paul Thompson NYU. Copyright Camera 32 2012.
